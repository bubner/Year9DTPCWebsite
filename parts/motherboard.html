INFORMATION 
            Connectivity between a computer's hardware parts, such as the CPU, RAM, hard drive, and graphics card, is provided by the motherboard.
            There are several types of motherboards made to accommodate various sizes and types of computers, and because
            of this, not every CPU and memory type is compatible with every motherboard.
            A motherboard also has the BIOS (Basic Input/Output System), a small chip on the computer that contains
            a small amount of information for the operating system kernel about what devices are currently connected to the motherboard.
DESCRIPTION 
            The MSI MEG Z690 GODLIKE is a high-end feature-rich E-ATX form factor motherboard designed for the purpose
            of the widest range of support and performance. It hosts a built in LCD touch screen for quick system info
            and tuning, and also has support for the latest standards such as WiFi 6E, 10G ethernet, and DDR5, just
            to name a few.
            
            This motherboard has LGA 1700 pins with four DIMM slots and two PCI-E x16 lane slots, alongside x5 M.2 SSD slots.
