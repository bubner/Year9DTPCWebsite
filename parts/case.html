INFORMATION 
                All of the crucial and pricey components of a PC or other computing device are protected by a computer casing. Internal components for modest desktop units,
                whole networks, and even sophisticated rackmount servers include items like the motherboard, chassis, discs, CPU, and more.
                The desktop computer case aids in shielding the parts from physical harm, invasive foreign objects, and electrical tampering. The case's
                design is crucial as well since differing forms might impact the airflow through the device, which is essential to keeping it cool and functional.
                The enclosures also provide space for expansion slots, warning lights, graphics cards, RAM, and other server applications while keeping the internal
                components isolated from one another. A computer case also gives style and aesthetic to all computer builds, which is a necessity
                considering the fragile parts of a computer are not all that good looking on their own.
